Potatoes Greens


Size = 310.00 kb | Resolution 1080x613

Potatoes-greens is a traditional dish popular to Montesrrado and Monrovia, in Liberia.
Potatoes-greens can be eaten with rice, it can be cook with either red oil or agro-oil.
Note: For this recipe , i will be using the agro-oil method.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Potatoes leaves.
  • Pepper
  • Fish, Crab, Crab-Fish or meat
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Seasoning
  • Vita
  • Oions or Garlic

Steps

  1. Wash potatoes leaves.
  2. Cut or slice into smaller pieces and add the slice the oion or garlic on top of it.
  3. Clean the fish, crab or meat and season it and steam it for 10 mins.
  4. After cutting the leaves, set the pot on the fire and pour the oil into it.
  5. Wait for 5-10 mins when the oil is hot.
  6. When the oil is hot , pour the slice leaves into the oil.
  7. When it is in the pot, add a little bit of baking soda to it.
  8. Let it fry for 10 mins, add the pepper and 2 vita
  9. After that , make sure the leaves looks a little dark green and add water .(your perference)
  10. Add the fish ,crab or meat and let it boil for 10 mins and add 2 more vita.
  11. Reduce the heat and let it boil slowly until you are sure all the water is dry out.
